The Muse of Meals

Story

Have you ever found yourself staring into the void known as the fridge, wondering what to make?

We all have these clueless moments, well our app is here to help! The Muse of Meals will help to inspire your inner-chef. Do you have a craving for a certian cusine or maybe a type of meat. Or are you the adventurous type and want to take a chance on something totally random? Muse of Meals will give you a random and delicious recipe while pair a tasty wine selection just for you. All you have to do is search the app, pour a glass of wine, enjoy the cooking and feasting on your next masterpiece!

This is a simple app that will help the lost soul to take back the kitchen, by providing inspiration to cook lovely meals.
